About FastPDFReader

FastPDFReader is simple and totally free online recreation of 'word by word' reader apps which allow users to read documents much faster by displaying them only one word at a time with chosen speed. It requires practice to get used to this kind of reading, but once done, can improve time of reading pretty intensively. Give it a try and maybe you will start run-reading books one day.
